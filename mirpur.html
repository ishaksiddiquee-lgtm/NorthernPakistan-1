About Mirpur

Mirpur is a city in Azad Kashmir, Pakistan. It is known as the "City of Pioneers" due to its large diaspora community spread across the globe, particularly in the United Kingdom. The city is surrounded by beautiful mountains and has a pleasant climate.

Mirpur is famous for its contributions to the UK's Pakistani community, with many families having connections to Britain. The city has beautiful landscapes, rivers, and is known for its hospitality. It serves as an important center in Azad Kashmir.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Mirpur is from April to October when the weather is pleasant. The region offers beautiful spring and autumn seasons.
