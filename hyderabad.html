About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, known as the "City of Colleges," is Pakistan's sixth-largest city located in Sindh province. The city has a rich history dating back to the 18th century and features several historical sites including the Pacca Qila and Makli Necropolis. Hyderabad is famous for its traditional crafts, especially pottery and lacquer work.

The city serves as an important educational center and has a diverse population with a unique cultural blend of Sindhi, Urdu, and other regional influences. Hyderabad's strategic location has made it an important trade and commerce hub throughout history.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Hyderabad is from October to March when the weather is cooler and more pleasant. The summer months can be quite hot.
