About Kohat

Kohat is a city in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is located in the Kohat District and has historical significance due to its strategic location. The city has been an important center throughout various periods of history.

Kohat is known for its historical forts and strategic importance. The city serves as an important commercial and administrative center in the region. It has a rich cultural heritage and is surrounded by mountains.

Key Attractions

  • Kohat Fort
  • Historical Sites
  • Strategic Location Views
  • Cultural Heritage Locations
  • Traditional Markets
  • Historical Mosques
  • Regional Museums
  • Mountain Scenery

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Kohat is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot.
