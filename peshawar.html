About Peshawar

Peshawar, the capital of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in South Asia. Located near the Khyber Pass, it has been a gateway to the Indian subcontinent for centuries. The city is known for its rich Pashtun culture, traditional bazaars like Qissa Khwani Bazaar, and historical sites including Peshawar Museum and Gor Khatri.

Peshawar is famous for its traditional crafts, cuisine, and as a center of Pashtun culture and literature. The city has played a significant role in the history of the region and continues to be an important cultural and economic center for the Pashtun community.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Peshawar is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The spring season (March-April) is particularly pleasant.
