About Rawalpindi

Rawalpindi, commonly known as Pindi, is a major city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is the fourth-largest city in Punjab and the ninth-largest in Pakistan. Rawalpindi is the administrative seat of the Rawalpindi District and is the military headquarters of the Pakistan Armed Forces.

The city has a rich history dating back to the 19th century when it served as an important garrison town during British rule. Today, it remains a significant military and commercial center. Rawalpindi is connected to Islamabad by the Islamabad-Rawalpindi twin city corridor.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Rawalpindi is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The city has a semi-arid climate with hot summers and cool winters.
