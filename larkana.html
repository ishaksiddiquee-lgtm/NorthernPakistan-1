About Larkana

Larkana is a city in the Sindh province of Pakistan. It is the birthplace of former Prime Minister Zulfikar Ali Bhutto and his daughter Benazir Bhutto. The city is located on the west bank of the Indus River and has a rich historical and cultural heritage.

Larkana is known for its connection to the ancient Indus Valley Civilization. The nearby archaeological site of Mohenjo-daro is one of the world's earliest major urban settlements. The city serves as an important commercial and agricultural center in Sindh.

Key Attractions

  • Bhutto Mausoleum
  • Mohenjo-daro Archaeological Site
  • Shah Rukn-e-Alam Shrine
  • Indus River
  • Larkana Museum
  • Shah Abdul Latif University
  • Historical Mosques
  • Traditional Bazaars

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Larkana is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be extremely hot.
