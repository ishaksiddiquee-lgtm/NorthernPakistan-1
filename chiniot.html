Discover the city of woodwork and craftsmanship

About Chiniot

Chiniot is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is known for its woodwork and craftsmanship, particularly in creating intricate wooden furniture and decorative items. The city is located on the banks of the Chenab River and has a rich cultural heritage.

Chiniot is famous for its traditional woodworking industry, which produces high-quality furniture with intricate designs. The city has a rich history and is known for its contributions to the arts and crafts of Punjab.

Key Attractions

  • Woodwork and Furniture Workshops
  • Chenab River
  • Historical Mosques
  • Traditional Craft Centers
  • Local Markets
  • Chiniot Fort
  • Cultural Heritage Sites
  • Handicraft Exhibitions

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Chiniot is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot.
