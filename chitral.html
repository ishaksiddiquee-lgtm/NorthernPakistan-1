Discover the land of ancient traditions and high peaks

About Chitral

Chitral is a district in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. Located in the northernmost part of Pakistan, Chitral is known for its unique culture, languages, and traditions. The region is surrounded by some of the world's highest peaks, including Tirich Mir, the highest peak in Pakistan outside of the Karakoram range.

Chitral has a rich cultural heritage with unique customs and traditions that differ from the rest of Pakistan. The region is known for its hospitality, traditional music, and festivals. The Kalash people, who practice an ancient polytheistic religion, live in the Chitral district.

Key Attractions

  • Tirich Mir Peak
  • Kalash Valleys
  • Chitral Fort
  • Shandur Polo Ground
  • Madakhlasht Valley
  • Bumboret Valley
  • Chitral Gol National Park
  • Ancient Archaeological Sites

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Chitral is from May to September when the weather is pleasant and the roads are accessible. This is the peak season for tourism and outdoor activities.
