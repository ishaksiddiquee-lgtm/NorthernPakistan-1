About Mall Road

Mall Road is the central promenade and commercial hub of Murree, Pakistan. This historic road was established during the British colonial period and remains the heart of the city's social and economic activity.

The road is lined with colonial-era buildings, shops, restaurants, and hotels that reflect the area's rich history. Mall Road offers visitors a chance to shop for local handicrafts, enjoy traditional food, and experience the vibrant atmosphere of Murree.

During the day, Mall Road bustles with tourists and locals alike, while in the evening it transforms into a romantic setting with beautiful views of the surrounding mountains. The road is pedestrian-friendly and offers a pleasant walking experience with numerous photo opportunities.
