Skardu

Discover the land of high peaks and pristine lakes

About Skardu

Skardu is the capital of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. Located in the Karakoram range, Skardu is known for its stunning natural beauty, including some of the world's highest peaks, pristine lakes, and glaciers. The city is surrounded by K2, Gasherbrum I, and Gasherbrum II peaks.

Skardu is a popular destination for mountaineering, trekking, and adventure tourism. The region is home to the famous Shangrila Resort, Lower Kachura Lake (Shangrila Lake), and Deosai Plains. The area has a rich cultural heritage and diverse ethnic groups.

Key Attractions

  • Shangrila Resort & Lake
  • Deosai Plains
  • Kachura Lake
  • Shigar Fort
  • Skardu Fort
  • Sheosar Lake
  • Habibullah Lake
  • Thalay Lake

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Skardu is from May to September when the weather is pleasant and the roads are accessible. This is the peak season for trekking and mountaineering activities.
