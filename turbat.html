About Turbat

Turbat is a city in the Balochistan province of Pakistan. It is the capital of Kech District and serves as an important center in the Makran division. The city is located in the central part of Balochistan and has a strategic location in the region.

Turbat is known for its proximity to the Makran Coastal Highway and serves as a gateway to the beautiful coastal areas of Balochistan. The region has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its traditional crafts and hospitality.

Key Attractions

  • Gateway to Makran Coast
  • Coastal Highway Access
  • Traditional Baloch Culture
  • Local Markets
  • Regional Handicrafts
  • Historical Sites
  • Traditional Cuisine
  • Desert Landscapes

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Turbat is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be extremely hot.
