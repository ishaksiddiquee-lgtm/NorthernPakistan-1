About Gilgit

Gilgit is the capital city of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. Located in the northern part of Pakistan, Gilgit is known as the gateway to the Karakoram, Hindu Kush, and Pamir mountain ranges. The city is surrounded by some of the world's highest peaks, including K2, the second-highest mountain on Earth.

Gilgit is a popular destination for mountaineering, trekking, and adventure tourism. The region has a rich cultural heritage and diverse ethnic groups. The city serves as a base for expeditions to various peaks in the area.

  • Hunza Valley

  • Gilgit Fort

  • Passu Cones
  • Hussaini Suspension Bridge

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Gilgit is from May to September when the weather is pleasant and the roads are accessible. This is the peak season for trekking and mountaineering activities.
