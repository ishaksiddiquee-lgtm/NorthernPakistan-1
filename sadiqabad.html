About Sadiqabad

Sadiqabad is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is located in the Rahim Yar Khan District and is known for its agricultural significance and industrial development. The city is named after Sir Sadiq Muhammad Khan Abbasi, the former ruler of Bahawalpur State.

The city serves as an important commercial and agricultural center in southern Punjab. Sadiqabad is known for its rice production and other agricultural products. It has also developed into an industrial hub with various manufacturing units.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Sadiqabad is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot.
