About Islamabad

Islamabad, Pakistan's capital city, is a planned city nestled at the edge of the Margalla Hills. Designed in the 1960s to replace Karachi as the capital, it features wide boulevards, well-planned sectors, and modern infrastructure. The city is known for its green spaces, low crime rate, and organized layout.

Key landmarks include the Faisal Mosque, Pakistan Monument, and Daman-e-Koh. Islamabad is ranked among the most livable cities in Pakistan and offers a high quality of life. The city seamlessly blends modernity with Pakistani culture and heritage.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Islamabad is from October to April when the weather is pleasant. The spring season (March-April) is particularly beautiful with blooming flowers.
