About Hattu Lake

Hattu Lake is a beautiful alpine lake located in the Hunza Valley of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. Situated at a high altitude, the lake is surrounded by majestic mountains and pristine forests, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

The lake is known for its crystal-clear waters that reflect the surrounding peaks and sky, creating a mesmerizing sight. The area around Hattu Lake is ideal for picnics, photography, and enjoying the tranquility of nature away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Visitors to Hattu Lake can enjoy the serene environment, take leisurely walks around the lake, and experience the natural beauty of the Hunza Valley. The location offers excellent opportunities for relaxation and connecting with nature in its purest form.
