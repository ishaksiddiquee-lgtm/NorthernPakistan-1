Jhang

Discover the city of artisans and historical significance

About Jhang

Jhang is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is known for its pottery, handicrafts, and cultural heritage. The city is located on the banks of the Chenab River and has a rich history dating back centuries. Jhang is also known for its literary figures and poets.

The city serves as an important agricultural and commercial center in central Punjab. It is well-known for its pottery industry, particularly blue pottery, and traditional crafts. Jhang has produced many notable personalities in literature, politics, and sports.

Key Attractions

  • Blue Pottery Workshops
  • Chenab River
  • Shrine of Hazrat Shah Jewna
  • University of Jhang
  • Historical Mosques
  • Local Handicraft Centers
  • Chenab Riverside
  • Traditional Bazaars

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Jhang is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot.
