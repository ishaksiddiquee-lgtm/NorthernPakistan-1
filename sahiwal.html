Discover the agricultural hub of Punjab

About Sahiwal

Sahiwal is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is the 35th most populous city in Pakistan and the 9th most populous city in Punjab. The city is known for its agricultural significance, particularly for the Sahiwal breed of cattle, which is one of the most important dairy breeds in the world.

Sahiwal has a rich history and cultural heritage. The city serves as an important commercial and agricultural center in central Punjab. It is well-connected to other parts of Pakistan via road and rail networks.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Sahiwal is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The city experiences a semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters.
