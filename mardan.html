About Mardan

Mardan is a city in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is the 6th largest city in KPK and is known for its historical significance and cultural heritage. The city is located in the Mardan District and has been an important center in the region for centuries.

Mardan has a rich history and is known for its connection to various historical events and figures. The city serves as an important commercial and educational center in the region. It has several historical sites and cultural landmarks.

Key Attractions

  • Historical Sites
  • University of Mardan
  • Cultural Heritage Locations
  • Traditional Markets
  • Historical Mosques
  • Local Handicrafts
  • Regional Museums
  • Commercial Areas

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Mardan is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot.
