Discover the city of mangoes in Punjab

About Sargodha

Sargodha is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is known for its mangoes and is sometimes called the "City of Mangoes." The city is an important industrial and commercial center in central Punjab. Sargodha is also known for its furniture industry and agricultural products.

The city has a rich history and cultural heritage. It serves as the administrative center of Sargodha District. The city is well-connected to other parts of Pakistan via road and rail networks.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Sargodha is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The summer months can be quite hot, especially during mango season (May-June).
