About Abbottabad

Abbottabad is a city in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is located in the Hazara region and is known for its pleasant climate and scenic beauty. The city was founded in 1853 by Major James Abbott and is surrounded by mountains and pine forests.

Abbottabad is famous for its natural beauty, pleasant weather, and as a major educational center with several universities and colleges. The city is also known for its connection to the US military operation that led to Osama bin Laden's death in 2011.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Abbottabad is from April to October when the weather is pleasant. The city offers cool summers compared to the plains.
