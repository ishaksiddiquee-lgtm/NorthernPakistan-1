About Gujranwala

Gujranwala is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is the seventh-most-populous city in Punjab and the eleventh-most-populous city in the country. The city is known for its industrial sector, particularly textiles, food processing, and paper mills.

Gujranwala has a rich history and cultural heritage. The city is well-connected to other parts of Pakistan via road and rail networks. It serves as an important commercial and transportation hub in northern Punjab.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Gujranwala is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The city experiences a semi-arid climate with hot summers and mild winters.
