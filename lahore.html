About Lahore

Lahore, known as the "City of Gardens," is the capital of Punjab province and Pakistan's cultural heartland. Famous for its Mughal-era monuments, including the Badshahi Mosque and Lahore Fort, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The city is renowned for its food, poetry, literature, and art.

Lahore's Walled City features the iconic Lahore Gate and numerous historical sites. The city has a rich history dating back over a millennium and is considered one of the most important cultural centers of South Asia. The annual Basant festival and the rich tradition of qawwali music make Lahore a vibrant cultural hub.

Key Attractions

  • Lahore Fort (Shahi Qila)
  • Shalimar Gardens

  • Minar-e-Pakistan
  • Ichhra Bazaar
  • Allama Iqbal Tomb

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Lahore is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and cool. The summer months can be extremely hot.
