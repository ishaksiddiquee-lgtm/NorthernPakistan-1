About Sheikhupura

Sheikhupura is a city in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is known for its historical significance and cultural heritage. The city is named after Sheikh Ibrahim, a Muslim saint. It is located about 45 km northwest of Lahore and serves as the capital of Sheikhupura District.

The city has a rich history and is known for its agricultural significance. Sheikhupura is also famous for its sports goods industry and furniture manufacturing. The city has several historical sites and cultural landmarks.

Key Attractions

  • Sheikhupura Fort
  • Killa Sheikhupura
  • Qila Sheikhupura
  • Sheikhupura Museum
  • Shrine of Data Sahib
  • Chata Mali
  • Sheikhupura Stadium
  • Historical Mosques

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Sheikhupura is from October to March when the weather is cooler. The city experiences a subtropical climate with hot summers and cool winters.
